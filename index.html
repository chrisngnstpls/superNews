Hedgehog Achieves Remarkable Feat, Graduates College Blindfolded

In an astonishing display of determination, a resilient hedgehog has graduated from college while blindfolded, defying expectations and captivating onlookers. The hedgehog, named Sonic, utilized specialized techniques and support to navigate coursework despite the visual impairment.

"Sonic's achievement is a testament to adaptability and spirit."
- Dean Jane Smith

College officials praised Sonic's perseverance. Sonic plans to pursue environmental advocacy, inspiring others with his journey. This unique graduate's story is a beacon of triumph, proving that obstacles can be overcome with resolve and creativity.
