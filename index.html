Texas Erupts in Chaos: ‘Rabid Horde’ Sparks Downtown Apocalypse

Residents fled in terror as a group of individuals described as “rabid, screaming figures” rampaged through downtown Dallas yesterday, smashing storefronts and allegedly biting bystanders in what witnesses called a “real-life zombie outbreak.”

Police in riot gear and hazmat suits swarmed the scene, detaining nine suspects after a three-hour standoff. “They were foaming, wild-eyed—like something from a horror movie,” said shaken barista Elena Rodriguez.

Authorities confirmed the group had untreated medical conditions but denied “rabies” claims. The motive remains unclear, though conspiracy theorists cite a viral TikTok trend. Governor Abbott vows to “hunt down every accomplice.”
